Gravitation teams, is a plugin to display teams on your site, with ShortCodes

== Description ==
Gravitation teams, is a plugin to display teams on your site, with ShortCodes, includes a carousel to display images, responsive.
Go to GV. teams , Add New Team complete the form with name, social links, then add a feature Image and your done.
Later go to a post , page or widget and add the shortcode [gravitation_teams].

== Installation ==
Download and install from WordPress.org.
Configure as you see fit, under Gravitation / Gravitation teams.
1. Upload \'gravitation-teams.php\' to the \'/wp-content/plugins/\' directory
1. Activate the plugin through the \'Plugins\' menu in WordPress
1. Place [gravitaiton_teams] in your templates
